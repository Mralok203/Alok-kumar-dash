    OUR BRANCHES




    CSE/IT

    HOD- REETANJALI PANDA

    Computer Science Department was started to meet the demand of trained manpower in both software and hardware sector. The Department has computers of latest version in three air conditioned spacious computer center. The Department has latest licensed software for training purpose. The department has a maintenance wing to repair and maintain computers of the institute.

    ETC

    HOD- DEBI PRASAD PATNAIK

    epartment of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering under UCPES, Berhampur, is a skill manpower training center to meet the challenge of Digital India. The department has Communication Engineering Laboratory, Microprocessor Laboratory, Digital Laboratory, Electronics Circuit Laboratory and Circuit Theory Laboratory to train the students. All these laboratories are fully furnished with tools and equipments.

    CHMICAL

    HOD- ER.SATYA SANKR RAJ

    Chemical Engineers are the backbone of process industries. The Department is equipped with 07 modern laboratories with latest equipment's. All faculty of the department have work experience in industrial organization .industrial organization. .

    BIOTECH

    HOD- SWATANGINI NAIK

    The Department of Biotechnology was started in the year 2006. Biotechnology is the technology of 21st Century with ample opportunity in Agriculture sector to pharmaceutical sector. This department is First of its kind in India with a successful placement in reputed tissue culture and pharmaceutical company. There are three laboratories with latest equipment and training kit for students. .

    ELECTRICAL

    HOD- SIDARTH SANKR SAHU

    The department of Electrical Engineering was started in the year 1958. The department was set up keeping in view of the increased demand of the industry and it able to fulfill the same. At present the department is equipped with Electrical Machine Laboratory, Electrical Measurement Laboratory and Electrical Workshop Practice Laboratory..

    CIVIL

    HOD- ER.MR NAYAK

    Civil Engineering Department has come a long way since its inception in this institution, under Private organization with a modest start in the year 1956. Since then it has come a long way in catering to the need of human resources in various Government, Public sector and private organizations as well.

    MECHANICAL

    HOD- ER.ANIL KUMAR PATRA

    The Mechanical Engineering branch is one of the pioneer branches which is started since inception of the institution. The department has well developed laboratories equipped with instruments of generations to the latest one. The major laboratories are, Heat Power Laboratory, Hydraulic Machinery, Materials Testing Laboratory, CNC Laboratory.
